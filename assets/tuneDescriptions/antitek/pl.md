This tune is very easy to learn and yet groovy. It can be played very fast.

**Sign:** place your hands as if you were holding a telescope over one eye and
rotate your hands as if to zoom in and out.

## Kontekst

*To be written.*

## Poziom trudności

* **Surdos:** easy
* **Repi:** łatwy
* **Snare:** easy
* **Tamborim:** medium
* **Agogô:** easy
